About Me

I am a Self-Driven Problem Solver and Innovator who loves to take on challenges to push myself to do better. Using my cognitive skills, I always try to think of efficient ways to do things. Being naturally curious, I always ponder upon how things work and question if they can be made better. I am a quick grasper and can swiftly adapt to highly dynamic teams and start contributing by delivering valuable output. Always excited and fascinated with what technology can achieve these days and try to think of ways businesses can leverage technology to create products that have a positive impact on society.

Over the past years as a Software Developer, I have learned numerous tools and programming languages and specialize in UI/UX. I have worked in an Agile team and have delivered high-quality SaaS products before deadlines and without a reduction in the project scope. Having assisted the Product Owner in Stakeholder Interactions and Scrum Ceremonies, I found a deep interest in the responsibilities they had and decided to pursue a Master's Degree to help me transition into a similar role.

I have completed my Master's in Information Technology Management from The University of Texas at Dallas graduating as a Scholar of High Distinction and am on the path to be a successful Product Manager.
